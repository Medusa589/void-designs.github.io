About Me

Who Am I?

I'm Kelly Hewitt—a creative at heart with a deep love for nature, technology, and artistic expression. I channel all three into everything I create, blending natural inspiration with digital tools to craft meaningful, visually compelling work.

What Do I Do?

I create visuals that support compelling stories, blending artistic thinking with technical know-how. I work across disciplines and industries—solving problems, supporting teams, and helping turn ideas into reality.

How Do I Do It?

As a CG & VFX generalist, I bring a broad skill set across industry-standard tools, combining technical proficiency with a creative edge. I also enjoy developing custom tools to streamline workflows and optimize production pipelines.

Where Am I?

I currently reside in the beautiful capital city of Aotearoa, Wellington. I’m also open to remote work or relocating.
