About Me

Who Am I?

I'm Kelly Hewitt - a creative at heart with a deep love for nature, technology, and artistic expression. I channel all three into everything I create, blending natural inspiration with digital tools to craft visually compelling work.

What Do I Do?

Skills & Future Focus

Technical

As a Technical Director by trade, I'm well-versed across major VFX platforms and pipelines, including Katana, Maya, and Nuke. I approach each project as an opportunity to challenge myself and push the boundaries of what's possible in computer graphics. From developing custom pipeline tools to optimising complex render workflows, my focus is always on delivering innovative, technically sound solutions. Notable work includes Avatar, Transformers, and Rebel Moon which required complex scene and render optimisation to keep within budget while still having an extremely high quality output. I'm continually expanding my skillset to stay ahead in this fast-evolving industry.

Artistic

With a deep passion for visual storytelling and self-expression, I strive to create work that resonates with each audience on an emotional level. I enjoy shaping narratives through imagery, using design, colour, and composition to enhance story and tone. One of my most notable contributions was to the Avatar series, where I translated abstract creative direction into tangible visuals that met (and often exceeded) client expectations. My approach blends intuition with a strong understanding of visual language, allowing me to deliver work that not only meets the brief but also elevates the story. I often work with tools like Katana, Maya, and Houdini to bring ideas to life with both artistic depth and technical precision.

Atention to detail

Starting out as an Assistant Technical Director and progressing quickly into more advanced roles, I've developed a sharp eye for detail, both in the technical and artistic aspects of my work. I actively seek constructive feedback, but I also apply a constant layer of self-critique, always asking: “How can I push this further?” This mindset drives me to research emerging tools, workflows, and techniques, especially in areas where I see untapped potential. My attention to detail not only ensures a high level of polish in my output but also strengthens my problem-solving and creative decision-making across disciplines. It’s this commitment to refinement that often leads to cleaner, more efficient results in both production and final renders.

Future Focus

Curiosity is a core part of who I am. I’m always looking to understand how things work and how they can be improved. While my main focus is in film and VFX, I often branch into other industries, gaining new perspectives and ideas that shape my approach. Lately, I’ve been exploring emerging tools like real-time rendering and AI-assisted workflows, which I see as key to the future of storytelling. Learning these technologies not only keeps my skills sharp but also expands what I can offer creatively and technically.

Specialized Technique

I specialize in developing custom tools and render optimization workflows that balance creative freedom with technical efficiency. On projects like Avatar, I built solutions to streamline complex scenes and reduce render times, allowing for faster iteration without compromising quality. My ability to bridge artistic intent with smart technical systems has proven valuable not just in film, but across other industries where adaptable, efficient pipelines are in high demand.

Cross-Disciplinary Skill

Technical expertise alone isn’t enough. Strong communication is essential to understanding client needs and collaborating effectively. Throughout my career, I’ve made it a priority to develop not only my technical skills but also how I communicate with teammates and stakeholders. This balance fosters trust and clarity, resulting in smoother workflows and better, more efficient outcomes for every project.
